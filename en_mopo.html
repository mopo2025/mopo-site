Our Values

Honesty & Integrity We keep our promises and honor the trust placed in us by teammates and students. Mutual Support No one stands alone — we communicate openly and lift each other up Safety First Strength is not in showing off, but in protecting ourselves and those beside us. Empathy We embrace diverse backgrounds and remain open to learning from one another. Free Self‑Expression Ideas, feelings, and individuality are valued — always with mutual respect.

Coaching Principles

 Safety is our foundation. On the mountain, trust and confidence are built. Every individual’s path is respected. Each step of growth is supported with honesty. Diversity is embraced — in culture and values alike. A community of learning is created together. Nature deserves lasting respect. The beauty — and responsibility — of the mountains is carried forward to the next generation. Skills and mindset are refined without end. The standard of instruction keeps rising.

MOPO’s Beliefs

MOPO is a name that embodies beauty, reflecting the light of nature and the hues of snow. From family and culture comes the ethic of helping others proactively.” We cherish the honesty and trust within it. We believe that through the ski school, people meet, acknowledge one another, and spread small circles of goodwill— moving toward a future of social harmony and trust.
