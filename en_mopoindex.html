About MOPO

Why Choose MOPO Ski School?

1. Officially Licensed & Certified

We are a fully registered Japanese company with official teaching permits from the ski resorts. Enjoy a safe, secure, and high-quality lesson experience with total peace of mind.

2. Small Groups & Tailored Instruction

We specialize in small group lessons to ensure focused attention for every student. Our instructors adapt the pace and content to your fitness level and progress, creating the most effective learning experience for you.

3. Multilingual Support

Designed for international travelers. We offer instruction in English, Chinese, Cantonese, and Japanese. Learn without language barriers in a relaxed and friendly atmosphere.

4. Photos & Videos Included

We capture your best moments and learning progress during the lesson. You'll receive high-quality photos and videos afterwards — perfect for memories and technique analysis.
