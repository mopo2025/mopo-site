Sapporo

Sapporo Teine

Snowy peaks, just minutes from the city.

Perched on the western edge of Sapporo, Teine combines the convenience of the city with the serenity of nature. Just a short ride from downtown, it’s often called Sapporo’s own “backyard mountain.”

On bluebird days, the upper slopes open to sweeping views of the skyline and Ishikari Bay. The resort’s well-groomed, varied terrain caters to every rider—from absolute beginners and growing intermediates to seasoned skiers chasing steeper lines. Families will also find a dedicated kids’ zone, making Teine a welcoming spot for everyone—friends, couples, and children alike.

— Resort overview — by train & bus — by car

Resort overview

Teine has 15 trails spread evenly across all levels—about 30% beginner, 40% intermediate, and 30% advanced. First-timers can find their feet with ease, while experienced riders have plenty to push their limits. Two ungroomed powder runs keep the snow wild and playful for those chasing fresh turns.

On the slopes you’ll see a mix of roughly 60% skiers and 40% snowboarders, and the resort is also a hub for local school programs. Up top there’s a terrain park with jumps and rails, perfect for progressing freestyle skills. In the Olympia zone, a dedicated kids’ park welcomes little ones from as young as four, making it an easy pick for families.

Whether you’re new to the sport or a seasoned rider, Teine has something for everyone—so every visit feels like your own snow adventure.

Getting here by train & bus

✦ JR train + local bus

From New Chitose Airport or Sapporo Station, take JR to Teine Station, then transfer to a JR Hokkaido Bus. You’ll be on the slopes in about 16 minutes—a quick, hassle-free trip.

Click to enlarge

✦ Hotel shuttle “Big Runs” (reservation required)

A direct shuttle links major Sapporo hotels with Sapporo Teine. Choose a package that includes a 7-hour lift pass with round-trip bus, or simply book the bus on its own.

Getting here by car

Driving is easy, with free parking for around 2,800 vehicles.

  • About 60 minutes from New Chitose Airport (via expressway)
  • About 40 minutes from central Sapporo
  • About 40 minutes from central Otaru
  • About 10 minutes from Teine IC on Sasson Expressway
