Legal Operation Statement

August 15, 2025

Legally Qualified and Professional Instructors

All MOPO Ski School instructors hold valid residence status in Japan and internationally recognized ski teaching licenses. Strictly following Japanese regulations, they combine extensive experience and solid skills to deliver safe and high-quality lessons.

A Registered Corporation in Japan

MOPO Ski School is operated by “Godo Kaisha MOPO,” an officially established and registered company in Japan. We comply with the law, uphold integrity and professionalism, and continue to provide reliable services.

Our Commitment to Customers

Through this statement, we aim to help you better understand MOPO’s operating system and philosophy. We will continue to provide safe, secure, and rewarding learning experiences for students who love skiing and snowboarding. Please feel free to contact us with any questions.
