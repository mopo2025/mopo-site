DAY 01 SAPPORO

Arrival at New Chitose & Embracing the Sapporo Night View

„ÄêTheme: City Lights & Panoramic Heights„Äë

  • New Chitose Airport: Upon arrival, take the comfortable JR Rapid Airport train directly to central Sapporo.
  • JR Tower T38: Visit this climate-controlled observation deck connected to the station. Enjoy coffee from 160m up, overlooking the snow-covered cityscape.
  • Odori Park: After checking into your hotel, take a gentle evening stroll to admire the dreamy winter illuminations, a signature of the northern winter.

„ÄêTheme: Patient Instruction & Stress-Free Magic Carpet Practice„Äë

  • Afternoon Practice: Make good use of the "Magic Carpet (Snow Escalator)". It‚Äôs an effortless way to ascend beginner slopes without hiking, saving your energy for the fun part.
  • Dinner Recommendation: Nemuro Hanamaru or Toriton Sushi. Both offer fresh, high-quality ingredients and a rich variety that is a crowd-pleaser for the whole family. (Pro tip: Try the fried chicken at Toriton Sushi‚Äîit's surprisingly good!)

DAY 03 LEVEL UP

Breaking Through: Mastering the Art of Turning

„ÄêTheme: Technical Breakthroughs & Deep Training in the Olympia Zone„Äë

  • Morning: Consistent practice is key to progress. Utilize the excellent morning snow conditions to solidify your basics.
  • Afternoon: Challenge yourself with turning. Try the snowboard 'falling leaf' technique on your toe-side, or master ski snowplow turns to experience the joy of controlled steering.
  • Evening: Head to the lively Susukino district to sample essential Hokkaido dishes like Genghis Khan grilled lamb or the famous soup curry from GARAKU.

Evening travel to Susukino

  • Or walk via the "Chi-Ka-Ho" underground walkway (approx. 20 mins).

DAY 04 OTARU

Otaru Canal Stroll & One of Hokkaido's Top Three Night Views

„ÄêTheme: Nostalgic Charm & The Romance of Mt. Tengu„Äë

  • Daytime Stroll: Trade your ski boots for walking shoes and explore Otaru's historic red brick warehouses, glassblowing studios, and renowned confectionery shops like LeTAO and Kitakaro.
  • Night View: Take the ropeway up Mt. Tengu for a panoramic view ranked alongside Hakodate and Mt. Moiwa as one of Hokkaido's top three nightscapes.
  • ‚ö†Ô∏è Pro Tip: In the event of heavy snow suspending JR train services, consider taking a "Highway Bus" to Otaru as a reliable alternative.

  • Option B (Bus): Highway Otaru Bus (approx. 60 mins).

DAY 05 FINALE

Urban Aesthetics & Natural Wonders: The Grand Finale

„ÄêTheme: Tailored Itinerary ‚Äì Last-Minute Shopping vs. Scenic Onsen Escape„Äë

Option A: The Urbanite (Sapporo City)

Visit the "Sapporo Beer Museum" to sample historical brews and limited-edition beers in a Meiji-era red brick building. In the afternoon, head to the "Tanukikoji Shopping Arcade". It features Hokkaido's largest Don Quijote and various drugstores, and as it is fully covered, it's the ultimate destination for finalizing your souvenir shopping regardless of snow.

Option B: The Naturalist (Lake Shikotsu)

Explore "Lake Shikotsu", famous for having some of Japan's most pristine water and its unique "Shikotsu Blue" color. In winter, the magical "Ice Festival" features massive ice sculptures created by spraying water from the lake, spectacular under blue skies or evening lights.

Don't rush to immigration! Locals often joke that "New Chitose Airport is actually Hokkaido's best theme park". Be sure to arrive 3-4 hours early; it features hot springs, a movie theater, and even an on-site chocolate factory.

  • üç´ Royce' Chocolate World: Watch the production process through glass windows and buy exclusive items.
  • üéÄ Hello Kitty Happy Flight: A vibrant and cute themed area and cafe that kids will love.
Airport Transport & Crucial Reminders

‚úàÔ∏è Getting to New Chitose Airport (CTS)
